Chocolate Cake recipe


Here is a fast and easy version of the classic ChocolateCake recipe. Enjoy!!!

Difficulty Preparation Cooking Total Time
Easy 10min 15min 25min

Ingredients

Black Chocolate: 200 g

Sugar: 150 g

Eggs: 4

Butter: 2 tbs

Flour: 400gm

Instruccions

1

Melt Chocolate and butter together

2

Mix the sugar, eggs, and the flour

3

Mix everything to get a smooth dough

4

Place on over for 45 min

5

Let air for 30 min
